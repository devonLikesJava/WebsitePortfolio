Team 3635 The Flying Legion

About the Team

"The Flying Legion" was established in 2010 and has only been improving year after year. This year we have 22 Members of the Robotics Team that are allocated to different teams such as the Electrical Team, Software Team, Mechanical Team, CAD Team, Safety Team, and the Practice Field Development Team.

My Role

I am the Vice President and also the Lead Programmer of the software group. As Vice President I oversee all the projects that are going on and make sure they are on task and meeting deadlines. I currently oversee the Practice Field Development Team, the CAD Team, the Safety Team, the Mechanical Team, and the Software Team and Electrical Team. As the Lead Programmer I am head of the Software Team and the main programmer, I am entrusted to write the program that operates the robot in Java and make it function. I also manage the code as well as the rest of the Software Team.

What Have I Accomplished

This year alone I have accomplished tasks such as converting our Labview code from last year to Java code for this year. Started from scratch programming the new swerve drive, as well as programming the autonmous. I have also experienced programming vision tracking, gyroscopes and encoders.
